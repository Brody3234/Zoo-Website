The Zebra Gazette
Zebra herd escapes Zebra Plains amid critical perimeter breach event
A herd of 8 Zebras escaped the Zebra Plains area of Hughson Zoo on March 2, 2025 in what zoo staff are calling a critical perimeter breach event. The breach was created when, sources say, a construction worker doing landscaping work in the enclosure suffered an encapacitating heart attack while operating a bulldozer, causing the bulldozer to be driven into the perimeter fence. This incident created a large breach in the fence which the zebras in question were quick to exploit before staff were able to seal the breach.
Beloved lion King Mulasa passes away in the night
Mulasa, widely regarded as the king of the zoo, died of stomach cancer on March 1, 2025 at the age of 13. The mighty and aged lion was diagnosed with the malady in late 2024 by zoo veterinarians, who subsequently prescribed medication in an attempt to eliminate the cancer. However, by February the veterinarians had shifted to giving Mulasa painkillers to ease his pain in his final days. He is succeeded by his only scion, Simla, age 5.
